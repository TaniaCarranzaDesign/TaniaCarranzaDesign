Zazil

2024

Description

Zazil, which in Mayan means “light”,” is a collection of lamps that embody clarity and energy. Inspired by the Mayan ball game, the collection merges tradition with contemporary design.

Design Process

The table lamp is manufactured using CNC machining and thermoforming, and is designed to be assembled from 15 mm wooden boards. Its circular garnet-red element evokes the flaming ball that marked the beginning of the game, symbolizing cosmic dynamism and the connection with the divine.

Programs

  • Fushion 360
  • Solid Edge
  • Autocad
