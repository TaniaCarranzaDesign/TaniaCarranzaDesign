Muuver

2025

Description

Muuver is a children’s product designed with a strong focus on ergonomics, safety, and character. Inspired by the strength and stability of a bull, it responds to children’s needs through a secure, comfortable, and expressive form.

Design Process

The design process was based on the study of children’s anthropometric measurements and concept exploration through sketching. The final product was developed using a combination of fabrication systems, including wood bending with a 30T press, 4-axis CNC machining, metal forming and welding, plasma cutting, and 3D printing.
