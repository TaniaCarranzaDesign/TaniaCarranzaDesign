About Me.

Welcome to my portfolio. Here, I share the way I think, design, and transform ideas into tangible outcomes. My name is Tania Carranza Ramírez, and I am a Product Designer with a strong interest in thoughtful, adaptable, and responsible design.

Born in Guadalajara, Mexico, I have lived in three different countries and studied across five educational institutions. These experiences have shaped my ability to adapt quickly, observe carefully, and approach design from a global perspective. Travel and cultural exploration play an important role in my creative process, constantly influencing how I understand users, contexts, and materials.

I am currently completing a Bachelor’s Degree in Product Design at the University of Design, Innovation and Technology. My work focuses on modular and sustainable design, where functionality, aesthetics, and environmental awareness intersect. I approach each project with discipline, visual sensitivity, and a strong attention to detail, aiming to create meaningful and well-considered design solutions.
