Alana

2025

Description

Alana is a product designed for Woodendot in collaboration with Denver Karami. Rooted in balance and calm, it follows a clean, minimalist aesthetic inspired by Scandinavian design and the fluid geometry of a shark fin.

Design Process

Developed following Woodendot’s “Design with intention” philosophy, the project embraces sustainable principles through reduced forms, thoughtful material use, and timeless design. Alana ensures quality control, sustainability, and a close relationship between design and craft.
